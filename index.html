About Us

TeraFarm Backyard is a local farm dedicated to providing fresh, sustainable produce to our community. We believe in the power of nature and the importance of supporting local agriculture. Our mission is to grow healthy, delicious food while promoting environmental stewardship and community engagement.

Our Products

    Fast-growing, healthy, and farm-ready pigs raised to maximize your meat production and profit.

  • Piglets

    Strong, well-bred piglets with excellent genetics to help you build a productive and profitable herd.
